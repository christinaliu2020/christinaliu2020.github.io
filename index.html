About

I am a fourth-year Computer Science student at Caltech, where I am a member of the Caltech Vision Lab. I am also a member of the Stanford Translational AI Lab, and will be joining Stanford University as a PhD student in Computer Science in Fall 2026. I am supported by the Mellon Mays Undergraduate Fellowship for my research at Caltech.

My research interests lie broadly within AI systems and world models for biomedicine and neuroscience.
