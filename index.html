About Me

I am a senior at Caltech studying Computer Science. My research interests include machine learning, computer vision, medical imaging, and neuroscience. I am particularly interested in using machine learning to connect behavioral, neural, and imaging modalities to build multimodal, clinically deployable AI systems.
