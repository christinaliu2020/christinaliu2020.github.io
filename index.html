About Me

I am a fourth-year Computer Science student at Caltech and a member of the Caltech Vision Lab. My work spans machine learning, computer vision, medical imaging, and neuroscience, focusing on multimodal models that connect behavior, neural signals, and images for early disease detection and clinical deployment. I am grateful to be supported by the Mellon Mays Undergraduate Fellowship.
